About me

Junior web parogrammer & Network admin(CS)

My name is Akobir and I am 20 years old. I have been studying information security at the Tashkent University of Information Technologies named after Muhammad Al-Khorazmi for 3 years. I have been learning web programming online for 3 months now, I have written several articles on this subject on behalf of the cyber security faculty. Having basic knowledge of web programming, I want to go to an IT course in order to further strengthen this knowledge, I have IELTS level 7 in English. I work as an apprentice network administrator in the incubator room of the university where I study. My goal is to work as a network admin in Google.
